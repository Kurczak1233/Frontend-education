Why should you learn english?

Popularity  

English is the most popular language over the world!

Possibilities  

You will be able to speak to almost everyone. Learning english will make you be more knowledgeable and more elequent

Accessibility  

Most of the internet content is written in English. Once you learn it you will be able to read and watch almost everything in web!

Pleasure  

Many people derive immense pleasure from learning english. The more aware you are about your language, the better you feel!

Prestige  

The way you speak - especially in foreing language, makes you look more intelligent. This is definitely something which may impress your friends or colleagues!

Knowledge  

Not only will you learn the basics of English grammar, but also of other subjects! When you read a text you will have to understand it's merits which may broaden your horizons.

Development  

After a while, you will be able to take some English tests. If you pass those, you will be able to work up your way to the top in your company.
